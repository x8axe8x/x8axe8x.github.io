Front-End Development?

The internet in the modern age is the hub of all things knowledge, social, and entertainment. Anything on the web is accessible to billions of people all around the world and the tools available to today's developer make it easier than ever to get an idea out there. With devices like smart phones and tablets being the most prevalent means of surfing, content needs to be developed with mobile in mind but should still also scale up for desktop users. All this leads to a responsive approach, and as I see it, an interesting challenge.
